Welcome to North Hiking Adventures

Discover the beauty of Greece through unforgettable hiking experiences! At North Hiking Adventures, we organize guided hiking trips to carefully selected destinations across Greece. From breathtaking mountain trails to scenic coastal paths, our goal is to help you explore nature in the most immersive way.

Founded by Panos, a passionate hiker with over 10 years of experience, our company is dedicated to providing safe, well-planned, and memorable hiking adventures. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced trekker, we have the perfect trail for you!

Join us on a journey through Greece’s stunning landscapes and make memories that will last a lifetime. Let’s hike together!

Our Services

Guided Hiking Trips

Explore Greece’s most breathtaking trails with our expert-led guided hikes. Each trip is carefully planned to ensure a safe and unforgettable experience. Whether you prefer mountain adventures, forest trails, or coastal paths, we have the perfect route for you.

Custom Hiking Experiences

Looking for a unique hiking adventure? We offer personalized hiking trips tailored to your preferences, fitness level, and schedule. Tell us what you’re looking for, and we’ll craft an experience just for you!

Multi-Day Trekking Tours

For those who crave a longer adventure, we offer multi-day trekking experiences that allow you to fully immerse yourself in nature. Enjoy scenic campsites, authentic local experiences, and a true connection with Greece’s landscapes.

Hiking and Outdoor Skills Workshops

New to hiking? Want to improve your outdoor skills? Our workshops teach essential hiking techniques, navigation, survival skills, and safety measures to ensure you’re fully prepared for your next adventure.

Equipment Rentals and Recommendations

Don’t have the right gear? No problem! We provide high-quality hiking equipment rentals, including backpacks, trekking poles, and camping gear. We also offer expert recommendations on what to bring for each trip.
