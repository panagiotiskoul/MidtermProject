Create your Checklist

The Create Your Checklist feature helps you organize and prepare for your hiking trip by allowing you to build a personalized checklist of essential gear. You can select the trip date, specify the type of item, set its quantity, assign a priority level (High, Medium, or Low), and add a description for each item. Once your list is created, you can easily manage it by marking items as completed and filtering them based on priority or completion status, ensuring you stay organized and ready for your adventure.
